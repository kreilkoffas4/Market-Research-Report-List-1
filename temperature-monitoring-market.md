<p><ul><li>Non-Contact Temperature Sensors</li><li>Contact Temperature Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The temperature monitoring market comprises two main types: non-contact and contact temperature sensors. Non-contact temperature sensors use infrared technology to measure the temperature of an object from a distance, making them ideal for applications requiring hygiene or fast readings, such as in healthcare and manufacturing. In contrast, contact temperature sensors require direct contact with the object being measured, providing accurate readings for various industrial processes and environmental monitoring. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring safety, efficiency, and quality across multiple sectors.</p></p>
