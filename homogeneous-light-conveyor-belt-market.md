<p><p>The homogeneous light conveyor belt market is characterized by two primary manufacturing processes: calendering and coating. The calendering process involves compressing and shaping materials through heated rollers, resulting in a uniform, flexible belt ideal for various applications. In contrast, the coating process applies a specialized layer onto a base material, enhancing its properties like durability and friction resistance. Both methods aim to produce high-quality conveyor belts that cater to diverse industry needs, ensuring efficient material handling and transport.</p></p>
