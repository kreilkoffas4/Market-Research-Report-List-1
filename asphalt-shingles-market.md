<p><p>The asphalt shingles market includes three main types: high-performance laminated, laminated, and three-tab shingles. High-performance laminated shingles feature multiple layers for enhanced durability and aesthetics, offering superior resistance to harsh weather and a longer lifespan. Laminated shingles provide a balance of quality and cost, featuring a layered design that mimics the look of more expensive materials. Three-tab shingles are the most basic option, consisting of a single layer that is lightweight and budget-friendly, making them a popular choice for many homeowners.</p></p>
