<p><strong>The Optical Fiber Temperature Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dot Type Temperature Sensor</li><li>Distributed Type Temperature Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical fiber temperature sensor market features two primary types: dot type and distributed type sensors. Dot type temperature sensors measure temperature at specific points along the fiber, providing localized readings ideal for applications such as industrial processes. In contrast, distributed type sensors utilize the entire length of the fiber to deliver continuous temperature data over extended distances, enabling real-time monitoring across large areas, such as pipelines or power plants. Both types leverage the advantages of optical fibers for precision and resilience in challenging environments.</p></p>
