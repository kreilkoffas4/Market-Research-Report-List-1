<p><p>The Lithium Niobate crystals market is primarily categorized into Optical Grade and Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) Grade crystals. Optical Grade Lithium Niobate is utilized in advanced photonics applications, including telecommunications, modulators, and laser systems due to its excellent optical properties.
