<p><ul><li>Scraper</li><li>Brush</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Belt cleaning equipment is essential in various industries to maintain the efficiency and hygiene of conveyor systems. The market primarily features two types: scrapers and brushes. Scrapers are designed to remove bulk materials and sticky residues from the belt’s surface, ensuring minimal carry-back and system contamination. Brushes, on the other hand, use bristles to gently sweep away fine particles and dust, promoting optimal belt performance and extending its lifespan. Both types contribute to operational efficiency and reduced maintenance costs.</p></p>
