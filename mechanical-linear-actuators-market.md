<p><p>The mechanical linear actuators market comprises various types, including screw actuators, cam actuators, and wheel and axle actuators. Screw actuators convert rotational motion into linear motion using a screw mechanism, suited for precise positioning. Cam actuators utilize a rotating cam to produce linear movement, often employed for automated systems. Wheel and axle actuators facilitate linear motion through the rotation of wheels connected to axles, offering efficient movement in applications like material handling. Each type serves distinct industrial needs.</p></p>
