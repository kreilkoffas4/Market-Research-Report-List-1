<p><p>The automobile seat ventilation system market consists of two main types: blow and suction systems. Blow systems use fans to direct air into the seat, providing immediate cooling by circulating air. In contrast, suction systems draw air away from the occupant, promoting a cooling effect by reducing the temperature of the seat surface. Both types enhance passenger comfort, particularly in hot climates, by maintaining a pleasant climate within the vehicle, leading to increased demand for innovative seating features in modern automobiles.</p></p>
