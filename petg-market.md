<p><p>The PETG market is categorized into three main types: Extruded Grade PETG, Injection Molding Grade PETG, and Blow Molding Grade PETG. Extruded Grade PETG is typically used for films, sheets, and profiles, known for its transparency and strength. Injection Molding Grade PETG is designed for producing rigid parts and components, offering excellent clarity and toughness. Blow Molding Grade PETG is specifically formulated for creating hollow objects, such as bottles and containers, combining durability with lightweight properties ideal for packaging applications.</p></p>
