<p><ul><li>Dry Type</li><li>Wet Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The V-Belt Clutch market is primarily divided into two types: dry type and wet type. Dry type clutches operate without lubrication, relying on friction for engagement, making them suitable for applications with minimal environmental exposure. In contrast, wet type clutches are immersed in oil or other fluids, allowing for better cooling and reducing wear, which enhances durability in high-stress environments. Both types serve distinct industrial needs, from automotive to machinery, ensuring optimal performance in varied operational conditions.</p></p>
