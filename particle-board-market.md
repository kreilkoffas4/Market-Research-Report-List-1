<p><p>Particle board is an engineered wood product made by compressing wood particles, such as sawmill shavings, wood chips, or sawdust, along with adhesives and resins. The particle board market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand in the furniture industry and the growing trend of eco-friendly materials. As consumers become more environmentally conscious, manufacturers are adopting sustainable practices, such as using recycled wood and eco-friendly adhesives, which is further boosting market growth.</p><p>Additionally, the rise in urbanization and the expansion of the construction sector are contributing to the demand for particle board, as it is a preferred material for interior applications.
