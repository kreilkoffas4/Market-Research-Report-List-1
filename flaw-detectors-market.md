<p><ul><li>Magnetic Flaw Detectors</li><li>Ultrasonic Flaw Detector</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flaw detectors market comprises various types, including magnetic flaw detectors, ultrasonic flaw detectors, and others. Magnetic flaw detectors utilize magnetic fields to identify surface and near-surface defects in ferromagnetic materials, making them suitable for welding and fabrication applications. Ultrasonic flaw detectors employ high-frequency sound waves to detect internal flaws, offering precise evaluation for a wide range of materials. Other types may include eddy current, radiographic, and acoustic emission detectors, each serving specialized industries and applications in quality assurance and maintenance.</p></p>
