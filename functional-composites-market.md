<p><strong>The Functional Composites Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Metal Matrix Composites</li><li>Polymer Matrix Composites</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Functional Composites market includes two main types: Metal Matrix Composites (MMCs) and Polymer Matrix Composites (PMCs). MMCs combine metals with materials like ceramics to enhance strength, thermal stability, and wear resistance, making them ideal for aerospace and automotive applications. In contrast, PMCs consist of polymers reinforced with fibers or particles, offering lightweight, corrosion resistance, and flexibility, primarily utilized in construction, consumer goods, and electrical applications. Both types improve material performance and expand application possibilities across various industries.</p></p>
