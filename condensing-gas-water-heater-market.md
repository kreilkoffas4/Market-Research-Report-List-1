<p><p>The condensing gas water heater market includes two primary types: natural gas and liquid propane. Natural gas heaters are typically connected to a municipal gas supply, providing a consistent energy source for efficient heating. They are favored for their lower operational costs and minimal environmental impact. Conversely, liquid propane heaters are often used in areas lacking natural gas infrastructure. They offer portability and flexibility, making them ideal for residential and commercial applications. Both types emphasize energy efficiency and reduced emissions.</p></p>
