<p><p>Heated humidifier devices are categorized into two main types: stand-alone and integrated. Stand-alone humidifiers operate independently, allowing users to control humidity levels in individual rooms or spaces. These units are portable and versatile, ideal for personal or localized use. In contrast, integrated humidifiers are built into larger systems, such as HVAC systems, providing centralized humidity control for entire buildings. This type ensures uniform humidity distribution and is often more energy-efficient for larger spaces, catering to commercial and residential needs.</p></p>
