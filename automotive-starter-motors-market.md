<p><ul><li>Direct Control</li><li>Electromagnetic Control</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive starter motors market is segmented into two primary types: direct control and electromagnetic control. Direct control systems utilize a simpler mechanism for engaging the starter motor, typically involving a direct connection to the battery without additional components. In contrast, electromagnetic control systems employ solenoids to engage the starter motor, providing more precise control and reliability. Both types cater to different vehicles and performance requirements, influencing factors like efficiency, power consumption, and installation complexity in modern automotive applications.</p></p>
