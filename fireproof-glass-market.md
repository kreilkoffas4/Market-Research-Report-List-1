<p><p>The fireproof glass market includes several types: </p><p>1. **Wired Glass** features a wire mesh embedded within, enhancing strength and preventing shattering. </p><p>2. **Ceramic Glass** is known for its high resistance to thermal shock, providing excellent insulation.  </p><p>3. **Laminated Glass** consists of layers that bond together, offering safety and sound insulation while resisting fire.  </p><p>4. **Tempered Glass** is heat-treated for increased strength and durability, making it suitable for high-stress environments while maintaining fire resistance. Each type serves specific safety and design needs across various applications.</p></p>
