<p><strong>The Fiberglass Reinforced Grating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Compression Molding Type</li><li>Extrusion Molding Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Fiberglass Reinforced Grating market primarily consists of two types: Compression Molding and Extrusion Molding. Compression Molding involves placing resin and fiberglass into a mold, applying heat and pressure to form a solid structure, ideal for creating custom shapes and high-strength applications. In contrast, Extrusion Molding involves forcing heated resin mixed with fiberglass through a die, producing continuous sheets or shapes, which are easier to manufacture and suitable for large-scale applications. Both methods offer distinct advantages in durability and design flexibility.</p></p>
