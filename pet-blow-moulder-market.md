<p><strong>The PET Blow Moulder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Extrusion Blow Moulding Machine</li><li>Injection Blow Moulding Machine</li><li>Stretch Blow Moulding machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PET blow moulding market comprises three primary types: </p><p>1. **Extrusion Blow Moulding (EBM)**: This technique involves extruding a parison that is then inflated into a mould. It is efficient for producing large and hollow containers.</p><p>2. **Injection Blow Moulding (IBM)**: This method combines injection and blowing processes, creating precise and intricate designs, ideal for smaller, complex containers.</p><p>3. **Stretch Blow Moulding (SBM)**: In this process, preforms are heated and stretched before being blown. It produces strong, lightweight bottles, commonly used for beverages. Each type serves distinct applications based on design and production requirements.</p></p>
