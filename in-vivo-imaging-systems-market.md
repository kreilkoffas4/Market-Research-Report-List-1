<p><p>The in vivo imaging systems market encompasses various technologies that provide detailed internal imaging for diagnosis and research. Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) utilizes strong magnetic fields and radio waves for high-resolution images of soft tissues. Digital Angiography excels in visualizing blood vessels through enhanced imaging techniques. Ultrasound employs sound waves for real-time imaging, while Nuclear Imaging uses radioactive tracers to observe physiological functions. Optical Imaging (OI) captures images using light, often for molecular or cellular insights. The "Others" category includes emerging or less common technologies.</p></p>
